ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Cedric Amaya and I am a self-taught web developer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. My primary toolkit consists of HTML and CSS but I am continuously learning and soon I want to dive deeper into JavaScript and CSS preprocessors.


The biggest resource for my education has been freeCodeCamp, an open source community that teaches people how to code. The other major resource that has helped with my education of web development was HTML & CSS: design and build websites by John Duckett.


I am currently attending school and pursing an Associates to Transfer degree in Computer Science. After college, I would like to work as a front end developer and create the most beautiful websites!
